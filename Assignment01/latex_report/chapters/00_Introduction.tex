\section*{Introduction}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Introduction}
\label{cha:introduction}

This report presents a statistical analysis of an airplane dataset. We analyzed numerical and categorical variables to predict airplane prices and understand their relationships. 

In the first section, we imported the data, summarized variables like Price and Fuel Consumption, and performed t-tests and chi-square tests. In the second section, we analyzed the effect of the airplane Model, Sales Region, and Production Year on Price using one-way and two-way ANOVA. 

In the third section, we fitted Simple and Multiple Linear Regression (MLR) models to predict Price. We tested regression assumptions, applied transformations, and validated the final model using an 80/20 train-test split. In the final section, we applied Principal Component Analysis (PCA) to reduce dimensionality. We then fitted a Principal Component Regression (PCR) model and compared its performance against the MLR model.
